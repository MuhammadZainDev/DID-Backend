DuaonAI - Account Deletion

Last updated: May 2024

At DuaonAI, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can delete your account and associated data from our services.

Account Deletion Options

Option 1: Delete from the App

You can delete your account directly from the DuaonAI app:

  1. Open the DuaonAI app and log in to your account
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll down to find "Delete Account"
  4. Tap "Delete Account" and follow the confirmation steps

Option 2: Send Deletion Request by Email

If you prefer to request deletion via email, you can send a request to:

Email: duaonai.official@gmail.com

Please include your account email address in your request.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your DuaonAI account, we remove the following information:

  • Your account information (name, email, password)
  • Your saved favorites
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

Data Retention

We may retain certain anonymized data for analytics purposes, but this cannot be linked back to you personally. Some information may be retained in our backups for a limited period, but these are automatically deleted according to our regular backup rotation schedule.

Need Help?

If you're having trouble deleting your account or have questions about data deletion, please contact us:
